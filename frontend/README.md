MindHive 🧠
An HCI-Based Smart Home Automation and Healthcare Monitoring System

MindHive is an intelligent assistive healthcare platform designed to enhance the independence, safety, and quality of life of individuals with severe mobility impairments. By combining Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Internet of Things (IoT), and cloud technologies, the system enables users to interact with their environment through eye-blink-based controls while providing caregivers with real-time access to critical health information.

The platform integrates smart home automation, continuous vitals monitoring, emergency alert management, and caregiver support into a unified ecosystem, reducing dependency on physical assistance and enabling proactive healthcare management.

Overview

Individuals affected by paralysis often face challenges in performing routine activities and communicating urgent needs. Existing solutions generally focus on either assistive automation or healthcare monitoring, resulting in fragmented user experiences.

MindHive addresses this challenge by providing a centralized platform that combines:

Assistive appliance control through eye-blink interaction
Continuous physiological monitoring
Intelligent emergency alert generation
Remote caregiver supervision
Secure cloud-based health record management

The system is designed to promote autonomy, improve patient safety, and support efficient caregiving.
